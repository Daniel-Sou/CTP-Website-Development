UM Alumni Advisory Network

“UM Alumni Advisory Network” is a platform for students to interact with alumni and learn from their successful stories in graduate study and career. With the help of our enthusiastic alumni, students can know more about overseas graduate study and career information through the following activities:

  • Experience-Sharing Session by Alumni
  • Tea with Alumni
  • Alumni Online

Alumni Online

In order to provide students with first-hand information on overseas graduate study and career, some of our alumni are willing to be voluntary online advisors to answer students’ enquiries via emails, such as:

Graduate study:

Career:
  • Selection of university and major;
  • Preparation for graduate study;
  • Financing graduate study;
  • Campus life and supporting services for international students;

 
  • Possible career paths after graduation
  • Work environment and market trend
  • Advice on career planning and development
  • Points to note about working overseas
